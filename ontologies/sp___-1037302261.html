SituationPrivacy vocabulary (Draft Version)


Abstract : SituationPrivacy (SP) vocabulary addresses privacy concerns during the description of things that would be part of a user-driven situation.


Situation privacy representation formats: SP.owl , SP.json , SP.n3 , SP.JSON-LD , SP.ttl , SP.xsd , SP.rdf
